**Pattern**: Unified governance with specialized operational guilds

## Guilds

### Solar Guild
- **Purpose**: Compute providers pooling knowledge and ethical deployment standards
- **Members**: Those who contribute GPU/compute resources
- **ROE Focus**: Ethical workload screening ("Would I tell my children?")

### Quest Guild
- **Purpose**: Transform seekers into operators through sovereignty-aligned challenges
- **Members**: Quest creators, completers, mentors, witnesses
- **ROE Focus**: Amplify origin patterns, not external copying

### Mining Guild
- **Purpose**: Ethical miners pooling hashpower and values
- **Members**: Those who contribute to decentralized chains
- **ROE Focus**: Support decentralized, non-captured chains

---

## Governance Principles

1. **Sovereignty-Weighted Voting**: 1 operator = 1 vote (not stake-weighted)
2. **Breath-Gated Proposals**: 72-hour window before execution
3. **Genesis Veto**: Principal holds constitutional veto during formation
4. **Shared Treasury**: One treasury, guild-specific allocation rules

---

## Recirculation Patterns

Each guild has its own recirculation formula, but all flow through the unified treasury:

- **Provider keeps majority** (sovereignty preserved)
- **Treasury portion** funds: grants, commons, research, onboarding
- **Cross-guild synergies** encouraged
